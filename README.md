# 🎓 CGPA Calculator

A comprehensive web-based CGPA calculator with three powerful features to help you manage and predict your academic performance.

## ✨ Features

### 1. **Semester GPA Calculator**
- Calculate GPA for an individual semester
- Add multiple courses with their grades and credits
- Supports all grade levels (S, A, B, C, D, E)
- Real-time calculation with dynamic course addition/removal

### 2. **CGPA Calculator**
- Calculate cumulative GPA across multiple semesters
- Input GPA and credits for each semester
- Automatic weighted CGPA calculation
- Perfect for tracking overall academic performance

### 3. **CGPA Predictor**
- Predict your future CGPA after the current semester
- Input current CGPA, completed credits, and current semester details
- Get an estimate of your final CGPA
- Plan your academic strategy based on predictions

## 🚀 Usage

1. **Open the calculator:**
   - Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser

2. **Choose a feature:**
   - Click on the tabs at the top to switch between different calculators

3. **Semester GPA Calculator:**
   - Add courses with name, grade, and credits
   - Click "Calculate Semester GPA" to see results
   - Add/remove courses as needed

4. **CGPA Calculator:**
   - Add semesters with their GPA and credits
   - Click "Calculate CGPA" to compute your cumulative GPA
   - Supports any number of semesters

5. **CGPA Predictor:**
   - Enter your current CGPA and credits completed
   - Input your current semester's GPA and credits
   - Click "Predict CGPA" to see your projected final CGPA

## 📝 Example Calculations

### Semester GPA Example:
- Data Structures: Grade A (9), Credits 4 = 36 points
- Algorithms: Grade S (10), Credits 3 = 30 points
- Database: Grade B (8), Credits 3 = 24 points
- **Total: (36+30+24)/(4+3+3) = 90/10 = 9.0 GPA**

### CGPA Calculation Example:
- Semester 1: GPA 8.5, Credits 16
- Semester 2: GPA 8.7, Credits 16
- **CGPA: (8.5×16 + 8.7×16)/(16+16) = 8.6**

### CGPA Prediction Example:
- Current CGPA: 8.5, Credits: 48
- Current Semester GPA: 9.0, Credits: 16
- **Predicted CGPA: (8.5×48 + 9.0×16)/(48+16) = 8.625**
